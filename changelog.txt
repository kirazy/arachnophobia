Version: 1.0.3
Date: 2020-08-18
  Features:
    - Changed core functions and settings; by default the Spidertron has hidden legs and laser-pointer feet
    - Players may select how many legs the Spidertron has, from 2 to 8, using the `Number of legs` setting; there may be minor changes to behavior, but in general the Spidertron should be able to traverse the same as before
    - Players may change the legs from being visible to `hidden` or the feet to `laser-pointers`, using the `Leg display style` setting
    - Players may choose whether to display dust particles when the Spidertron walks using the `Display dust particles` setting
    - Players may mute the legs of the Spidertron using the `Mute legs` setting
    - Players may choose from a selection of colors for the eyes using the `Eye color` setting
    - Four settings toggling primary, secondary, tertiary and shell banks of lights have been added
    - A temporary setting has been added to change the technology icon and item icons to a cute rock with googly eyes, while a more comprehensive set of icons are prepared
  Changes:
    - Adjusted the laser-pointer feet, reducing the scale to 0.75
